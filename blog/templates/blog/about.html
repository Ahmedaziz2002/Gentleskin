The studio

Gentleskin is built around restraint, clarity, and skin-barrier respect.

Editorial lens

Every routine and product note is written for sensitive, dry, and eczema-prone skin first. The goal is to reduce friction, remove noise, and keep recommendations calm.

What matters

We prioritize formula comfort, barrier support, fragrance sensitivity, and realistic routines that can be maintained when skin is reactive.

How products are framed

Affiliate links may appear when a product is relevant, but editorial judgment comes first. Recommendations are meant to be helpful shortcuts, not pressure.
